HR Portal Staff Guide
Everything you need to manage your leave and extra hours
2025 Edition
1
Signing In

Open the HR Portal link shared by your HR team. Click Sign in with Microsoft and enter your school email address and password. You will be taken directly to your personal leave dashboard. If you have trouble signing in, contact the HR team or your line manager for assistance.

2
Understanding Your Dashboard

When you log in you will see your complete leave position at a glance. There are three key areas to be aware of.

Annual Leave Card

Shows your total available days. This is your annual allowance, plus any days carried forward, plus any days earned from approved extra hours. For example: 20d allowance plus 2d from extra hours equals 22d total. This figure updates in real time whenever a request is approved.

Extra Hours Strip

Appears in orange when you have logged extra hours that have been approved by your line manager. It shows how many hours you have worked and confirms how many days have already been added to your leave balance above. No further action is needed on your part.

Other Absences

Sick leave, medical appointments, CPD and compassionate leave are recorded here for HR purposes only. These absence types do not reduce your annual leave allowance in any way.

3
How to Submit a Leave Request
1
Enter your start date and end date using the date fields on the left panel of your dashboard.
2
Tick the Half Day box if you only need part of a working day.
3
Choose the correct absence type from the dropdown. See Section 4 for a full guide to each type.
4
For Sick Leave, you may enter a brief optional reason (for example "Flu" or "Back pain"). This is for HR records only.
5
Click Submit Request. Your line manager receives an automatic email notification straight away.
6
You will receive an email confirmation once your request has been approved or declined.
Please note: If you try to book more leave than you have available, the system will display a clear warning message before you submit. Always check your balance before submitting.
4
What Each Absence Type Means
Absence TypeWhat it is and when to use it
Annual Leave Deducted Use for holidays, personal days and planned time away. Days are deducted from your annual allowance.
Extra Hours Worked Manager approval Use this to log hours you have worked beyond your contracted hours. This benefits the school by providing valuable flexibility when the team needs it most, and it benefits you by converting those hours directly into additional leave days. Your personal rate is set by HR — the default is 8 hours equals 1 day. Must be approved by your direct line manager.
Time Off in Lieu Manager approval Use this once you have earned credit through Extra Hours Worked and wish to take those days as leave. Your line manager will confirm the timing works for the school before approving. Must be approved by your direct line manager.
Sick Leave HR record only Record whenever you are absent due to illness. Does not affect your leave balance. You may add an optional brief reason for HR records.
Medical Appointment HR record only Record attendance at medical or dental appointments. Does not reduce your allowance.
CPD HR record only Record continuing professional development. Does not reduce your allowance.
Compassionate / Unpaid HR record only Recorded for HR purposes only. Does not reduce your leave allowance.
School Holiday Worked Term Time Manager approval For term time staff only. Log days worked during school holidays. This benefits the school by keeping operations running during holiday periods, and it builds your holiday working credit toward your annual target. Must be approved by your direct line manager.
Term Time Leave Term Time For term time staff only. For absences during the school term. Approved absences are added to your holiday working target for the year.
5
How to Log Extra Hours You Have Worked

When you work hours beyond your contracted hours, log them through the portal and your line manager will review and approve them. Once approved, those hours are automatically converted into leave days and added to your balance straight away.

1
In the Request Absence form, select Extra Hours Worked from the absence type dropdown.
2
An hours input box appears. Enter the number of hours worked, for example 4.5.
3
The system instantly shows you how many days that converts to at your personal rate.
4
Enter the date the extra work took place and click Submit.
5
Your line manager receives a notification to review and approve the record.
6
Once approved, those days appear immediately in your leave balance on the dashboard.
Extra Hours Worked and Time Off in Lieu requests must always be approved by your direct line manager. This ensures the school can plan cover effectively and that the arrangement works well for both you and the wider team. Your line manager may ask you to choose a timing for Time Off in Lieu that suits the school.
6
Viewing Your Absence History

The right-hand panel of your dashboard shows a complete record of every leave request and absence. Use the filter buttons at the top to view all records, pending requests, records by absence type, or leave already booked for the next holiday year. Records waiting for approval are shown in amber. Approved records are shown in green. Any sick leave reason you entered appears within the individual record. Leave approved for next year carries a clear "Next year" label and does not count against your current balance.

7
Good to Know
Your leave balance always reflects the latest approved records in real time. There is no need to refresh or wait for an overnight update.
A clear warning appears before you submit if you are running low or trying to book more days than you currently have available.
Leave approved for the next holiday year is clearly labelled with a Next Year badge and counts against that year, not this one.
Once extra hours are approved, the days are added to your balance straight away. No further action is needed on your part.
Pending requests show in amber throughout the portal so you can always see what is still awaiting a decision.
If you believe your balance is incorrect, speak to your line manager or the HR team. Do not submit duplicate requests while waiting.
8
Getting Support

For questions about your leave balance or a specific request, speak to your line manager in the first instance. For system access issues or technical problems with the portal, contact the HR team directly.
